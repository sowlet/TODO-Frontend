COURSE SEARCH

WHAT IS COURSE SEARCH?

Course Search is a tool designed to help Grove City College students efficiently plan their academic schedules.

It allows you to search for available courses based on various criteria and build a candidate schedule that fits your needs.

WHO IS IT FOR?

This software is specifically designed for Grove City College students who want to streamline the course selection process.

Whether you're a freshman or a senior, Course Search can help you find the courses you need and create a schedule that works for you.

HOW CAN I BEGIN?

To begin, simply sign up for an account or log in if you already have one.

Once logged in, you can start searching for courses based on days, time range, course code, or keywords.

Add potential courses to your candidate schedule and let Course Search handle the time conflict resolution gracefully.
